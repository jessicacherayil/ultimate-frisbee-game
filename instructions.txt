WELCOME to the ULTIMATE Frisbee Game!
The rules of the game are simple:
1. You are playing as a frisbee team against a computer generated frisbee team
[You are the blue team, the Wellesley Whiptails, while the opponent is the red team]
2. Your goal is to go across the field to score a point
3. Every time you get a correct answer your player will catch the disc and move up the field
4. Every time you get a wrong answer the opposite team catches the disc and moves up the field
5. The progress bar at the top of the game documents your progress on the field
6. If the progress bar reaches 100% you have scored a point! 
7. If it reaches 0% the opposing team has scored a point.
8. You can start another game after either scoring or losing a point.
HAVE FUN!!!
